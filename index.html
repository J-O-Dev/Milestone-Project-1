Who are we?

We are a BCAA-approved and EASA-certified flying school consisting of a small group of motivated instructors and support staff. We bring people closer to their dream of being in the sky.

What do we do?

We provide high-quality, affordable training for students of all ages and experience levels. We also provide aircraft rental to experienced club members.

Why join us?

Thanks to our small operation you will be welcomed into a family of highly professional instructors and students who strive to give their best every single day.
