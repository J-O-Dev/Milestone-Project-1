Our Story

Eagle Flying Club was founded in 2019 by 3 motivated young pilots aspiring to pass on their love and passion for aviation to the world. Since then we have grown to be a BCAA-approved ATO. We provide both theoretical and practical training on a PPL level.

Our Fleet

We operate an industry-standard fleet of 2 Cessna 182 and 1 Cessna 152 aircraft out of Brasschaat Airport. Our C182 aircraft are equipped with a Garmin G1000 so we can provide VFR, IFR and Night training. For more adventurous pilots we offer introductory acrobatic courses on our C-152K.
